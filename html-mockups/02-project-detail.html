LLaVA-1.5: Improved Baselines with Visual Instruction Tuning
作者Haotian Liu, Chunyuan Li, Yuheng Li, Yong Jae Lee
日期2024-01-15
分类cs.CV
引用数1,847
arXiv ID2401.02954
We present LLaVA-1.5, an improved large multimodal model that achieves state-of-the-art performance on 11 benchmark tasks. Our approach leverages visual instruction tuning to bridge the gap between vision encoders and large language models. We further propose several enhancements including higher resolution input, improved projection layers, and a refined training strategy that significantly boosts performance on visual reasoning tasks.
